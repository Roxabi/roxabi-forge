§ 02
Type system
Typography
Inter for body text, JetBrains Mono for code and labels, Outfit for display headlines. Three weights cover 95% of use cases.

Inter — Body & UI

Light 300 · Regular 400 · Medium 500 · Bold 700
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. Designed for readability on screens, Inter features open shapes, tall x-height, and excellent legibility at small sizes.

JetBrains Mono — Code & Labels

Regular · Medium · Bold

§ 03
Theme variants
Dark / Light Themes
Dark theme is the default — optimized for long coding sessions and reduced eye strain. Light theme available via data-theme="light" toggle.
Default

Dark Theme

Deep charcoal background #0d1117 with warm off-white text. Optimized for OLED screens and late-night coding sessions.

Accent colors pop against the dark canvas, creating visual hierarchy without harsh contrast.

Alt

Light Theme

Warm cream background #f5f2ec with dark slate text. Ideal for daylight environments and document-heavy interfaces.

Accent colors are slightly darkened to maintain visual balance against the lighter canvas.
